Panel upgrades and service improvements

We replace aging panels, correct labeling, improve grounding and bonding, and add whole-home surge protection where it makes sense. If the home needs a service upgrade, we coordinate with the utility and handle permits. You receive a clearly labeled directory and photos for your records.

Outlets, switches, and device repairs

Loose devices, worn receptacles, and intermittent connections are more than a nuisance. We replace devices with quality parts, correct poor terminations, and add GFCI or AFCI protection where it improves safety.

Lighting and fans

Lighting transforms a room. We install low glare recessed fixtures, level pendants, and quiet fans with proper bracing. We set color temperature that complements your finishes and add smart dimmers so scenes are one tap away.

EV charging stations

We verify service capacity, route a clean circuit, and mount the charger at a comfortable height. We add a disconnect when required and label clearly. We test with your vehicle and help set charging schedules.

Clean, functional remodeling that respects your routine

Tub to shower conversion
