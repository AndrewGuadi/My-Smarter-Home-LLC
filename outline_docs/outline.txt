Two funnels to capture every visitor
Urgent repair path
 Entry points: “Fix It Now” button, sticky mobile dock.
 Flow: pick problem category → brief checklist → upload photo → choose timeframe (today, 48 hours, next week) → show earliest slot → confirm by text.


Planned project path
 Entry points: service pages, “Plan My Project” buttons.
 Flow: pick scope and budget range → style choices → address → calendar for in-home estimate → soft quote range on screen plus email.


Core interactions and micro-UX
Sticky mobile dock: Call, Text, Book, Quote. Visible on every page.


3-step quote wizard: progressively disclosed fields, photo upload, city auto-detect, SMS verification, clear price ranges for common tasks.


Service chooser: search box with typeahead for “panel upgrade,” “EV charger,” “leak under sink,” “shower remodel.”


Before or after slider: on Projects and service pages. One slider above the fold.


Process band: Diagnose, Quote, Fix with simple icons and one line each.


Trust ribbon: HIC number, insured, payment types, “family owned.” Always visible near CTAs.


Reviews carousel: filter by service type and city. Include owner replies.


City smart content: auto-fills “serving York Haven and nearby” and surfaces the closest appointment window.


Availability peek: shows the next three openings inline before the calendar modal.


Inline FAQs: expanders tied to the service you are viewing.


Exit micro-CTA: if a user moves to close, offer “Text us a photo of the issue” with a tap-to-text.


Accessibility niceties: text size control, clear focus states, keyboard navigation, high contrast mode toggle.


Performance delight: instant page transitions, lazy-loaded media, subtle fade and slide reveals only when in viewport.

Components and design system
CTA styles: primary filled, secondary outline, tertiary text link


Cards: e1 or e2 elevation with ring borders and gentle hover


Typography: clear staircase for headings and body, tabular numerals for prices and times


Color: warm, trustworthy neutrals with subtle accent for electric or water states


Motion: small 150 to 250 ms transitions only on actionable elements


Icons: simple line icons that match copy and are screen reader labeled


Conversion hygiene
Place phone and text CTAs before any scroll on every page


Two step forms: capture name and phone first, then details


Social proof near every big ask


Clear price ranges on routine tasks, not on complex remodels


Promise response time and meet it
